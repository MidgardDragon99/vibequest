Privacy Policy for Vibe Quest

Last updated: December 22, 2025

Introduction

Vibe Quest ("the Game") is a single-player role-playing game. This privacy policy explains how we handle user information.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

Vibe Quest:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use analytics or tracking software
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not make any network requests

Data Storage

The Game may store game progress data locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser/app data

Third-Party Services

Vibe Quest does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Vibe Quest does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The Game is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
